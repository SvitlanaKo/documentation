Adding a new product
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

**WORK IN PROGRESS**

To add a new product into the master catalog:

1. Navigate to the **Product>Products** using main menu.
2. Click **Create Product**.

.. image:: /completeReference/img/Products/Products/ProductsCreate0.png
   :class: with-border

4. Place the product under the category or subcategory: select the location in the master catalog and click **Continue**.
5. Enter the following information that best describes the product for your target audience:

.. image:: /completeReference/img/Products/Products/ProductsCreate1.png
   :class: with-border

6. Set translation rules for a product name, description and short description. **LINK ME TO THE COMMON ACTIONS TOPIC**. (Click translation icon and provide translation for the localization you care about).

.. image:: /completeReference/img/Products/Products/ProductsCreateTranslation.png
   :class: with-border

7. Add fixed product prices. Note that fixed prices override the automatically generated price lists. **LINK ME TO THE COMMON ACTIONS TOPIC**. (click **Add**, select a price list specify quantity, units, price value and currency.)

.. image:: /completeReference/img/Products/Products/ProductsCreate2.png
   :class: with-border

8. Create shipping options. In the Shipping Options section, click **+Add Options** and provide unit, weight and weight measuring unit, dimensions (width, height, depth) and dimensions measuring unit and freight class. **LINK ME TO THE COMMON ACTIONS TOPIC**.
