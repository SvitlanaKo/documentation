Welcome to OroCommerce Documentation
------------------------------------

OroCommerce is an open-source Business to Business eCommerce application built with flexibility in mind and with most comprehensive set of features - corporate accounts, multiple organizations, websites and stores, high-performance content management system (CMS), personalized catalog management, multiple and customized price lists for your sales organization and multiple shppoing lists for your buyer&#8217;s organization - available out of the box.
