Delegate Account Management to a Customer User
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

You may want delegate some the customer user management capabilities to the customer users with administrator role by enabling *Account Management* permissions and capabilities. See `Customer User Role <./../CustomerUserRoles>`_ sectin for more information about permission and capability management.
